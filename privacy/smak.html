Privacy Policy

As Smak Cafe Eatery, we attach great importance to the privacy of our users. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal data is collected, used and protected when you use our website. Please read this policy carefully.

1. Information Collected
Smak Cafe Eatery may collect the following types of information:

Personal Information: Information required to place an order, such as name, email address, telephone number, shipping address.
Device Information: IP address, browser information and technical data collected through cookies.
Payment Information: Card information used for order payments is processed securely through payment providers. Card information is not stored on our site.

2. Use of Information
The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

Processing orders, delivery and receipt of payments.
Provide customer service and resolve problems.
To improve our services and optimize user experience.
To inform users about campaigns and special offers (if permission is granted).

3. Protection of Information
The security of your personal information is our top priority. Smak Cafe Eatery implements security measures that comply with industry standards to protect your personal data from unauthorized access, loss or misuse. In addition, payment information is managed by secure payment providers.

4. Sharing with Third Parties
Smak Cafe Eatery shares your personal information with third parties only in the following cases:

By couriers to fulfill the delivery of the order.
In line with legal obligations or judicial requests.

5. Cookies
Our website uses cookies to enhance user experience and analyze site traffic. Cookies are small files that are stored in your browser and provide you with a customized experience when you visit the site. You can manage or disable cookie settings in your browser.
